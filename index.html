About Me

My name is Frank Oley. I'm the founder of The Oley Way, a consulting firm specializing in mixology services, classes, and bar consultation.

I've been featured on Virginia This Morning on CBS, I Have a Story with Greg McQuade on CBS, and The Michael Artsis Show on BeTerrific, hosted by the five-time Emmy Award winner.

The Oley Way has been teaching courses internationally on bartending and mixology since 2011, and consulting since 2005.

It's Not the Only Way, It's The Oley Way.
